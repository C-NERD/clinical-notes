- #[[History of headache]]
	- #card
		- Generalized, continuous and non-radiating described as throbbing in nature and not associated with stress, noise and time of day
		- Severe enough to prevent patient from sleeping
		- Associated with hx of
			- Neck stiffness
			- Photophobia
